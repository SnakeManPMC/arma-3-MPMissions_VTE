Available Air Support

With an air assault due at X-ray in a few hours, we would prefer that these reinforcements do not reach their destination.

Stay close to the river, and avoid the NVA camp at all costs.

A few AirCav patrols are in the AO north of the river, looking for VC bunkers. You may hear them if they engage, but will be too far to help them, or for them to help you.

Misson Made by Nagual 2002

Uses the Spectating script v1.0

Your SEAL team will be infiltrating NVA territory today. Your target is an NVA convoy of infantry, moving from the jungles to this NVA camp.

A UH-1H crashed along the river during the night, with claymore cargo. As the cargo was ditched before the crash, there may be enough claymores around to set a deadly trap for the convoy.

Get to the site and set up an ambush, intercept the infantry column and prevent them from passing you. There is not much time.

Find some sampans. NVA are patrolling the river.

Locate the crashed UH-1H. Claymore crates should be in the area.

Ambush the NVA infantry convoy. Use claymores and suprise, try to cut their numbers by at least half.

Use the sampans to get to this LZ. Call in extraction with your units AN/PCR 25 radio. A UH1 will extract your team from there.

Air Support


Two teams are on standby at Plei Me. Call in these teams on your AN/PRC Radio if you get in trouble, especially when you ambush the marching infantry. If things go wrong, call in a team to help you get the job done, or cover your extraction.

1) Pink Team:
An OH-6A and AH1J will patrol your AO, looking for enemy. They will operate independant of your unit, but will stay near your general postion. If the ambush goes wrong, and the NVA troops flee or get away, call in this team to help clean up the scattered enemy.

2) Red Team:
Two AH1 Cobras will provide CAS. Call in the red team if you want your squad to direct the CAS. They will join you. The Cobras will take a few minutes to arrive.

Spectating Script v1.0

The spectating script allows you to spectate other players after you die instead of flying around as a seagull.


The script UI appears after you die, and its rather simple to use. The arrows at the bottom right are used to cycle between spectated objects and the name of the currently spectated object is displayed at the bottom left. The view menu can be accessed from the "toggle menu" button from top left of the screen, from where you can cycle between different camera views.


If the spectating script UI is still visible after the mission has ended (back at the select mission menu or at the pool for example) pressing ESC at the beginning of the next mission will close it.
